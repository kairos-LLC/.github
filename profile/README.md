# Kairos

**Know when they're free.**

Kairos is a private-by-design scheduling and availability app. See who's around, in real time, without the back-and-forth of "when are you free?" texts — and without handing over your personal data to do it.

---

## What Kairos does

- **See who's around, 24/7.** Check the availability of the people in your circle at a glance, any time of day.
- **Share your schedule with one-time codes.** Give someone visibility into your availability using a single-use code — no persistent account linking required.
- **Custom weekly patterns and shift cycles.** Model recurring schedules, rotating shifts, and irregular work patterns, not just a fixed 9-to-5.
- **Private by design.** Kairos uses **recovery keys** instead of traditional email/password accounts. There's no account database tying your identity to your schedule — just a key only you hold, used to restore access if needed.

## Privacy by design

Kairos is built around minimizing what we know about you. Recovery keys replace traditional accounts: there's no password to reuse or leak, and no standing account record beyond what's needed to restore your access. Schedule sharing happens through one-time codes you control, rather than open-ended connections.
