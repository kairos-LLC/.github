# Kairos LLC Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Kairos LLC organization and its repositories (`.website`, `.swift`,
`.github`, and any others under the Kairos LLC organization) a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community of internal contributors, and to
treat one another with professionalism and respect.

This Code of Conduct applies to an internal, access-restricted set of
repositories rather than a public open-source project, but the same standard
of conduct applies: everyone interacting in these repositories, associated
issue trackers, pull requests, and any other Kairos LLC-affiliated
communication channel is expected to follow it.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback, especially in code
  review.
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our
  mistakes, and learning from the experience.
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the
  overall team and product.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or
  advances of any kind.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political
  attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email
  address, without their explicit permission.
- Deliberately introducing malicious code, exfiltrating secrets, or
  attempting to undermine the privacy guarantees Kairos is built on (see
  `req.txt`) in bad faith.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Kairos LLC is responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of
acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in
response to any behavior that it deems inappropriate, threatening,
offensive, or harmful.

Kairos LLC has the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not
aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for
moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all Kairos LLC-affiliated spaces
(repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions), and also applies
when an individual is officially representing Kairos LLC in public spaces.
Examples of representing our organization include using an official Kairos
LLC email address, posting via an official social media account, or acting
as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

### Enforcement Guidelines

Kairos LLC will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the
consequences for any action deemed in violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Correction** — *Community Impact*: Use of inappropriate language or
   other behavior deemed unprofessional. *Consequence*: A private, written
   warning, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an
   explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate.

2. **Warning** — *Community Impact*: A violation through a single incident
   or series of actions. *Consequence*: A warning with consequences for
   continued behavior, including a period of no interaction with the people
   involved across affected repositories.

3. **Temporary Suspension** — *Community Impact*: A serious violation of
   standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior. *Consequence*: A
   temporary suspension from any interaction with, or contribution access
   to, Kairos LLC repositories for a specified period.

4. **Permanent Ban** — *Community Impact*: Demonstrating a pattern of
   violation, sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment, or aggression.
   *Consequence*: A permanent revocation of any contribution access to
   Kairos LLC repositories.
